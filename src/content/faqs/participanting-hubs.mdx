---
title: 'Do I need to participate in-person?'
highlight: true
category: ['participate']
---

While we have a series of hubs open during the weekend, you are free
to participate remotely from wherever you are located - your university
lab, your bedroom, or even your local cafe.

We strongly urge you try and meet with your teammates as best you can
during the weekend to collaborate - even if it's through a video call
through Zoom or Microsoft Teams.
