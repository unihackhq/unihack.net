---
title: "Can we work on our idea beforehand? Can we work on an existing idea?"
highlight: true
order: 4
category: ["projects"]
---

Your team must work on an original project during the weekend. You will not be allowed
to work on an existing project.

You are not allowed to start any development or design work before the event. This includes, 
but not limited to, code, graphics, sounds and 3D designs.

However, you may start doing research and/or ideation before the event.
