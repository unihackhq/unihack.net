---
title: 'What is a hackathon?'
highlight: true
order: 1
category: ['general']
---

A hackathon is a creative, time-limited competition where people collaborate
to work on a prototype that solves an issue.

Students are encouraged to come up with an idea, form teams, and then build out
said idea during the weekend. Your prototype can be in any format - a website,
a mobile app, a game, or even a piece of hardware. The most important thing is
that you have something presentable at the end of the event.
