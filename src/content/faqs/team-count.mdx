---
title: 'How many people can be in a team?'
highlight: true
order: 3
category: ['participate']
---

You must have between four and six people in your team. You cannot participate
as an individual.

You may be asked to merge with another to meet the minimum number requirement.

We reserve the right to relax this requirement during the weekend depending on
a particular team's circumstance or the number of participants during the
weekend. Please reach out to one of the organizers on Discord during the weekend
for guidance.
